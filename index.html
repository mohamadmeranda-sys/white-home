FAQ

Common
Questions

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?
+
Yes — our team arrives fully equipped with all cleaning products, mops, vacuums, and eco-friendly supplies. You don't need to provide anything.
What areas of Ajman do you cover?
+
We cover all areas within Ajman emirate including Al Nuaimiya, Al Rashidiya, Al Rumaila, Al Hamidiya, Al Jurf, Al Rawda, and more. If you're unsure, WhatsApp us and we'll confirm.
What is the difference between Hourly and Deep Clean?
+
Hourly cleaning (AED 25/hr) covers standard maintenance — all rooms, kitchens, bathrooms, and floors. Deep cleaning (AED 30/hr) includes everything plus inside appliances, behind furniture, window tracks, skirting boards, and hard-to-reach areas.
How does the monthly contract work?
+
You choose a plan starting from AED 500/month. We assign you a dedicated team, lock in your preferred schedule, and you pay monthly. Minimum 3-month commitment. Cancel with 30 days notice.
Can I book a same-day clean?
+
Yes! If you book before 10am, we can often arrange same-day service. WhatsApp us for the fastest response and we'll confirm availability immediately.
How do I pay?
+
We accept cash or bank transfer. Payment is made after the service is completed and you're satisfied. Contract clients pay at the start of each month.
